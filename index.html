DIY Rubber Ducky on Raspberry Zero W & more...

Welcome guys!
Today I will share with you my notes and walkthoughs on Howto make a Rubber-Ducky like with a Raspberry-Pi-Zero-W.

Hardware Specifications

Rubber Ducky:

-Fast 60 MHz 32-bit Processor
-Convenient Type A USB Connector
-Expandable Memory via Micro SD
-Hideable inside an in an innocuous looking case
-Onboard Payload Replay Button

This is a really tiny board but with an active community behind it:
Payload Wiki
Duck Toolkit
Simple Ducky Payload Generator
Ducky Decode
Rubber Ducky Forum
Hack5

Raspberry-Zero-W:

-802.11 b/g/n wireless LAN
-Bluetooth 4.1
-Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)
-1GHz, single-core CPU
-512MB RAM
-Mini HDMI and USB On-The-Go ports
-Micro USB power
-HAT-compatible 40-pin header
-Composite video and reset headers
-CSI camera connector

This is a more powerfull board, bigger too, Pi-Zero-W
